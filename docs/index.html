Research Mission

Project Lobster is an automated investment research system designed to analyze ETF markets, evaluate diversification benefits, monitor portfolio risks, and generate asset allocation insights.

The platform combines financial analytics, automation, and AI-assisted research workflows to support data-driven portfolio management.
